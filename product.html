Adee™

THE PROBLEM

50% of patients do not take their medication as prescribed

OUR SOLUTION

The Adee platform generates and acts on real-time data to improve adherence with zero patient burden

Product & Service Description

MedAdhere improves adherence by using AI-assisted behavioral change and remote monitoring through Adee™.

More than a medication bottle – The MedAdhere bottle arrives pre-filled with medication. It includes a precision scale that detects medication usage and transmits data automatically.

Adee™ knows how to help – The bottle data is analyzed by Adee, which sends personalized reminders via SMS to ensure adherence.

Fully Integrated System

Adee™ seamlessly integrates with healthcare systems, physician workflows, case management platforms, and analytic databases.

Business Model - Helping Payors Reduce Cost

Phase 1 (2025-2026) - Health Plans & Providers

Targets high-risk conditions with a significant link between medication adherence and hospitalization risk.

Simple per-patient fee with a pay-for-performance model.

Phase 2 (2027+) - Pharmaceutical Manufacturers

Helps pharma companies demonstrate value and increase revenue through improved adherence.

Phase 3 (2027+) - Clinical Research Organizations

Provides real-world evidence to support clinical trials, reducing costs and improving study quality.

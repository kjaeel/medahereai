Adee by MedAdhere AI

MedAdhere’s Adee platform is uniquely designed to deliver proven results with:

  • ✔ Zero patient burden
  • ✔ Proven adherence improvement
  • ✔ Payer-informed business model

How It Works

1. Smart Device

Medication-taking behavior is reported in real time via connected devices.

2. Behavioral Analysis

Activity patterns are analyzed and compared to prescribed medication schedules.

3. Intelligent Interventions

Automated SMS messaging is triggered to improve adherence and address barriers.

4. Clinical Visibility

Care teams and providers receive real-time dashboards to identify high-risk patients and triage effectively.

Adee Meets Patients Where They Are

Through a stratified risk approach, Adee identifies where patients fall along the adherence continuum and tailors support accordingly:

High Risk: Rapid identification and direct outreach.
Moveable Middle: Automated SMS interventions target this group to shift behavior.
Good to Go: Confirms sustained adherence and graduates members from further intervention.
